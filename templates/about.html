About Us

The idea of crime analyzer is to find areas with high crime rates and try to stop crime before it happens. Crime Analysis refers to the usage of mathematics, predictive analytics and other analytical techniques in law enforcement to identify potential criminal activity Studying and analyzing patterns in crime is used in various countries and organizations.

To be better prepared to respond to criminal activity, it is important to understand patterns in crime. In our project, we analyze crime data from a City, scraped from publicly available website of Police. At the outset, the task is to predict which category of crime is most likely to occur given a time and place in a City.
